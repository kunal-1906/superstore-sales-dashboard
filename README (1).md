# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard

An interactive business intelligence dashboard built to analyse sales performance across regions, categories, and time periods.

## 📌 Objective
To visualise and analyse sales data for a retail superstore, enabling data-driven decisions for revenue growth, profit optimisation, and operational efficiency.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **HTML5** — Structure
- **CSS3** — Responsive styling
- **JavaScript** — Data processing and interactivity
- **Chart.js** — Interactive charts and visualisations

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- **4 KPI Cards** — Total Revenue, Orders, Profit, Avg Order Value with YoY comparison
- **Monthly Revenue Trend** — Line chart showing seasonality and growth patterns
- **Revenue by Region** — Doughnut chart (North, South, East, West)
- **Revenue by Category** — Bar chart (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)
- **Profit Margin by Category** — Comparative margin analysis
- **Orders by Ship Mode** — Distribution across Standard, Express, Economy
- **Top 10 Products Table** — Ranked by revenue with performance badges
- **Interactive Filters** — Filter by Region, Category, and Year in real-time

## 📈 Key Business Insights
1. **Technology** is the highest revenue-generating category, contributing ~55% of total sales
2. **West region** leads in revenue driven by premium product sales
3. **Express shipping** has grown significantly post-2022, indicating customer preference shift
4. Average profit margin: ~25% across all categories
5. Q1 and Q3 show consistent revenue peaks — seasonal demand pattern
6. **Apple products** dominate the top 10 revenue list
7. Office Supplies show highest order volume but lowest average order value
